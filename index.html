Community Powered

An equity-driven bridge to higher education

CalDreams convenes Oakland schools, community colleges, and workforce partners to expand access to academic resources, financial literacy, and job-connected learning.

  • Family-centered coaching grounded in culturally affirming practices
  • Guided course mapping that accelerates transfer and degree completion
  • Mentor networks, internships, and apprenticeships with local employers
  • Transparent data dashboards that track student progress every term
