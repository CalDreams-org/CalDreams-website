Academic and career outcomes that shift what’s possible.

CalDreams delivers measurable results—higher course completion, stronger transfer rates, and improved employment outcomes for East Bay students and families.

Key Outcomes

Academic, transfer, and employment gains across our students

CalDreams participants outperform state averages at every milestone, with notable gains for first-generation, Black, and Latinx students navigating complex systems.

92%
First-to-second term persistence among coached students
68%
Participants completing transfer pathways in three years
3.2
Average GPA for students in academic support labs
87%
Graduates employed or in grad school within six months
Evaluation

Independent evaluations confirm our model works

CalDreams partners with research institutions to track outcomes and refine services so students receive the most effective academic and career support.

  • Third-party evaluations in partnership with UC Berkeley’s equity labs
  • Shared dashboards with East Bay Unified and Peralta Community College District
  • Public annual reports and community accountability sessions
