Home / Programs

Services that guide students from the classroom to their career.

Our integrated model combines academic support, transfer counseling, and career readiness so underserved students can access higher education and living-wage employment.

Academic Support

Strengthening grades and confidence in core subjects

We empower students with tutoring, mentoring, and learning resources that address academic gaps and accelerate mastery toward graduation requirements.

🧪

STEM & Writing Labs

After-school labs staffed by credentialed educators provide targeted support in math, science, and writing aligned to A-G course expectations.

  • Diagnostic assessments and goal-setting sessions each term
  • Individual learning plans co-created with students and families
  • Workshops on study skills, time management, and college readiness mindsets
👥

Family Academic Coaching

Coaches partner with families to interpret grades, advocate in school meetings, and connect students to dual-enrollment and credit recovery options.

  • Navigation of school resources, IEPs, and 504 plans
  • Culturally responsive workshops in English, Spanish, and Chinese
  • Bridge programming that prepares students for rigorous coursework
Transfer Counseling

Guiding students through high school, dual enrollment, and community college

Expert counselors design individualized transfer roadmaps, align course loads to CSU/UC requirements, and ensure students maximize financial aid.

🧭

Pathway Planning

Counselors map A-G coursework, Advanced Placement, and dual-enrollment classes to keep students on track for UC and CSU eligibility.

🗂️

Transfer Navigation

We coordinate with community college advisors to track units, complete TAG agreements, and submit timely CSU/UC applications.

💰

Financial Aid Coaching

Workshops and one-on-one support help students complete FAFSA/CADAA forms, interpret award letters, and access emergency resources that sustain enrollment.

Career Readiness

Connecting coursework to real-world experience

CalDreams bridges the gap between college and career with mentorship, internships, and industry-aligned training that reflects the goals of each student.

  • Career readiness bootcamps that build resumes, portfolios, and interview skills
  • Paid internships with civic, tech, healthcare, and skilled trades partners
  • Mentor matches with professionals who reflect student identities
  • Alumni network supporting job placement, apprenticeships, and graduate school
🌟

Career Accelerator Cohorts

Students participate in intensive cohorts that pair industry mentors, project-based learning, and stipends that make professional experience accessible.
