Our Programs

Comprehensive support from cradle to career

Cradle-to-Career Support

At CalDreams, we understand that educational success requires sustained support at every stage of development. Our four comprehensive programs work together to create a seamless pathway from early childhood through college and into meaningful careers.

Ages 0-5

Early Start

Building Strong Foundations

The Early Start program supports families with young children by providing resources and support during the critical early years of development. We believe that investing in early childhood education sets the stage for lifelong success.

Program Features:

  • College Savings Accounts - $500 seed funding for every enrolled family
  • Early Literacy Programs - Books, reading materials, and literacy workshops
  • Parent Education - Workshops on child development and school readiness
  • Family Support Services - Connection to community resources and support networks
  • Home Visiting Programs - Personalized support for families

Who Can Apply:

Families with children ages 0-5 living in California. Priority given to low-income families and first-time parents.

Grades K-12

K-12 Success

Academic Excellence & College Readiness

Our K-12 Success program provides comprehensive academic support, mentoring, and college preparation to help students excel throughout their school years and prepare for higher education.

Program Features:

  • Academic Tutoring - Free tutoring in math, science, and language arts
  • Mentorship Programs - One-on-one mentoring with trained professionals
  • College Readiness Workshops - SAT/ACT prep, college tours, application support
  • Summer Programs - Enrichment activities and learning opportunities
  • Family Engagement - Regular parent meetings and family events
  • Technology Access - Laptops and internet access for students in need

Who Can Apply:

Students in grades K-12 attending public schools in California. Programs available at partner schools and community centers.

High School & College

College Pathways

Making College Accessible & Achievable

College Pathways provides comprehensive support to help students navigate the college application process, access financial aid, and succeed in higher education.

Program Features:

  • College Application Support - Guidance through the entire application process
  • Scholarship Programs - Merit and need-based scholarships up to $10,000 per year
  • Financial Aid Workshops - FAFSA completion, financial literacy training
  • Campus Tours & College Fairs - Exposure to diverse higher education options
  • Essay Writing Support - Help crafting compelling personal statements
  • First-Generation Support - Specialized resources for first-gen students
  • College Success Coaching - Ongoing support throughout college years

Who Can Apply:

High school juniors and seniors, current college students, and recent graduates. GPA minimum of 2.5 and demonstrated financial need.

College & Young Professionals

Career Launch

From College to Career Success

Career Launch helps students and recent graduates transition successfully from college to meaningful careers through internships, job training, and professional development opportunities.

Program Features:

  • Internship Placement - Paid internships with local employers
  • Job Training - Industry-specific skills training and certifications
  • Career Counseling - One-on-one guidance from career coaches
  • Professional Development - Resume building, interview prep, networking skills
  • Employer Partnerships - Direct connections to hiring companies
  • Alumni Network - Ongoing support and networking opportunities
  • Leadership Development - Skills training for career advancement

Who Can Apply:

Current college students (sophomores and above), recent graduates (within 2 years), and young professionals seeking career advancement.
