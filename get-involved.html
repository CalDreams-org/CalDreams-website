Mentorship

Become a CalDreams Fellow mentor

CalDreams Fellows commit to a year of mentoring, offering guidance, networking, and encouragement that keeps students focused on academic and career goals.

📋

What mentors provide

  • Monthly check-ins with assigned scholars
  • Career coaching, resume review, and mock interviews
  • Connections to professional networks and apprenticeships
  • Celebrations of academic, transfer, and employment milestones
🚀

Training & support

Mentors receive orientations, ongoing training, and access to a resource hub with conversation guides, workshops, and staff support.
