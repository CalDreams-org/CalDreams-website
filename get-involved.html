Take the first step toward educational success

Who Can Apply?

CalDreams programs are open to students and families throughout California. We prioritize:

  • First-generation college students
  • Low-income families
  • Underrepresented communities
  • Students demonstrating academic potential

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application
  2. Submit required documentation
  3. Attend an information session (optional)
  4. Receive notification within 2-4 weeks

Important Dates

Early Start: Rolling enrollment

K-12 Success: Applications due August 1

College Pathways: Applications due November 1

Career Launch: Rolling enrollment
