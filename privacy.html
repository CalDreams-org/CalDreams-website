Privacy Policy

CalDreams is committed to safeguarding the personal information of our students, families, donors, and partners.

Information we collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and payment information associated with donations. For students and families participating in our programs, we may also collect academic and demographic data to provide services effectively.

How we use your information

CalDreams uses personal information to process donations, communicate program updates, provide academic support, transfer counseling, and career readiness services, and share impact reports. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Data security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. Sensitive data, including payment details, is processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant systems.

Third-party services

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who support our operations (e.g., email platforms, payment processors). These providers are obligated to maintain confidentiality and use data solely for the services they deliver.

Your rights & choices

You can update your contact information, unsubscribe from communications, or request that we delete your personal data by reaching out to privacy@caldreams.org.

Children’s privacy

For program participants under 18, CalDreams obtains parent or guardian consent in accordance with federal and state laws. We collect only the information necessary to deliver services.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated periodically. We will post revisions with an updated effective date and notify stakeholders of significant changes.
