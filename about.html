Our story, our purpose, our community.

CalDreams, Inc. is a nonprofit organization advancing academic support, transfer counseling, and career readiness for underserved students in Oakland.

Mission & Vision

We expand equitable access to higher education and meaningful careers.

CalDreams was founded by educators, counselors, and community advocates who saw too many promising students lose momentum between high school, community college, and employment. We align academic coaching, guided transfer pathways, and career-connected learning so every student gains the tools to thrive.

Our vision is an Oakland where first-generation and low-income students graduate with degrees, earn family-sustaining wages, and lead in their communities. Together with public agencies and employers, we deliver culturally responsive supports rooted in community expertise.

Core Values

Guiding principles for student-centered impact

CalDreams is rooted in collaborative leadership and community wisdom. These values guide how we design programs, steward resources, and measure success.

⚖️

Educational justice

We elevate the lived experience of Black, Latinx, Indigenous, and immigrant families to co-create solutions that close opportunity gaps.

🤲

Collective leadership

Our board, staff, and youth leaders share power in decision-making to ensure programs reflect community priorities and needs.

📈

Accountability with care

We track what matters, celebrate progress, and iterate quickly to ensure students receive responsive, high-impact support.
