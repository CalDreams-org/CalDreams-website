About CalDreams

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team making it happen

Our Mission

CalDreams empowers every student in California to achieve their educational and career goals by providing comprehensive support, resources, and opportunities from early childhood through college and into the workforce. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education and the tools needed to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Vision

We envision a California where every student has the support, resources, and opportunities needed to pursue higher education and meaningful careers. Through cradle-to-career support systems, community partnerships, and dedicated advocacy, we're building a future where educational equity is a reality and every child can achieve their dreams.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CalDreams began with a simple but powerful idea: that every student deserves a pathway to success, regardless of their zip code or family income. What started as a small scholarship program has grown into a comprehensive cradle-to-career initiative serving thousands of students across California.

Our founders recognized that supporting students at just one point in their educational journey wasn't enough. True transformation requires sustained support from early childhood through college and into careers. That's why we developed our four-pillar approach: Early Start, K-12 Success, College Pathways, and Career Launch.

Today, CalDreams partners with schools, community organizations, businesses, and families to create a robust support ecosystem. We've awarded over $2 million in scholarships, served more than 5,000 students, and built a network of mentors, educators, and community leaders dedicated to educational equity.

Our work is driven by the belief that talent is universal, but opportunity is not. We're committed to changing that reality, one student at a time.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Equity

We believe every student deserves equal access to quality education and opportunity, regardless of background or circumstances.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families, schools, and organizations to create a supportive ecosystem for students.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards and continuously improve our programs to better serve students.

Empowerment

We provide students with the tools, resources, and confidence to take control of their educational journey.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and approaches to address evolving educational challenges and opportunities.

Accountability

We measure our impact, learn from our results, and remain transparent with our stakeholders.
